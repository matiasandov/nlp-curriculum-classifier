On 13 October 2014, Morrison got his fifth BMI Million-Air Award for 11 million radio plays of the tune Brown Eyed Girl making it one of the Top 10 Songs ever on US radio and TV. Morrison has additionally gotten Million-Air grants for Have I Told You Lately 

The Songwriter's Hall of Fame declared on 8 April 2015 that Morrison would be the 2015 beneficiary of the Johnny Mercer Award on 18 June 2015 at 

their 46th Annual Induction and Awards Dinner in New York City. 

On 4 February 2016 he was knighted, by Prince Charles, for administrations to the music business and to the travel industry in Northern Ireland. 

In 2017, it was reported that the Americana Music Association would respect Van Morrison with the Lifetime Achievement Award for Songwriting at its September Honors and Awards service. 

Morrison was picked to be respected by Michael Dorf at his yearly philanthropy show at Carnegie Hall. The Music of Van Morrison was performed on 21 March 2019 by twenty melodic acts including Glen Hansard, Patti Smith and Bettye LaVette.
